Who is this for?

Anyone who is curious about what goes into building a tabletop game and the game design mechanics that make them work. If you’ve been thinking about making a board game, remixing a favourite, or just enjoy learning something new, you should attend this workshop.

What will be covered?

  • An introduction to basic elements of game-design (mechanics, actions, goal, narrative)

  • You will come up with a narrative with the help of randomly distributed picture cards(which we’ll provide)

  • We’ll add a layer of action points and goals on top of this storyline and implement one single mechanic (from a list of 10 basic ones)

  • You’ll test the new game against 5 random lenses (We’ll be using the deck of lenses by Jesse Schell)

  • Making a pitch for the refined game

What is the takeaway?

Participants will have their very own prototypes by the end of the workshop, which they can build on, refine, and play with others.
